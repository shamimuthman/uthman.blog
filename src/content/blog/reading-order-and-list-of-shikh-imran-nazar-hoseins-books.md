# Sheikh Imran N. Hosein’s Books: Reading Order

Sheikh Imran N. Hosein’s official e‑books page and bookstore listing include a comprehensive list of his works. In particular, the core **eschatological sequence** should be read in the following order:

1. **Jerusalem in the Qur’ān** *(foundation of the story)* . This book introduces the prophetic role of Jerusalem.
2. **Sūrah al-Kahf and the Modern Age**. A modern commentary on Sūrah 18, building on the Jerusalem theme.
3. **An Islamic View of Gog and Magog in the Modern World**. Discusses the end-time roles of Yājūj and Mājūj.
4. \*\*Dajjāl, the Qur’ān and Awwal al-Zamān\*\* *(The Beginning of History)* . Explains the emergence of the Antichrist **(Dajjāl)**  in history.
5. **The Qur’ān, Dajjāl and the Jasad**. Continues the discussion on Dajjāl and his *“corpse”* **(jasad)** .
6. **Constantinople in the Qur’ān**. Covers the prophecy of the Muslim conquest of Constantinople before Dajjāl appears.
7. **The Qur’ān, the Great War and the West**. Places the First World War and Western powers in the end-time context.
8. **Explaining Israel’s Mysterious Imperial Agenda**. Analyzes modern Israeli politics in Qur’anic prophecy.
9. **Madīna Returns to Center-Stage in Ākhir al-Zamān**. Shows how, after Constantinople’s conquest, Madīna becomes the focus *(preventing Dajjāl’s entry)* .
10. **In Search of Khidr’s Footprints in Ākhir al-Zamān**. Explores the role of Khidr *(a spiritual guide figure)*  in the End Times narrative.
11. **The Messiah, The Qur’ān and Akhir al-Zaman** (aka *From Jesus the True Messiah to Dajjāl the False Messiah*). This is the final, comprehensive work tying all themes together.


After the core sequence, the following **supporting titles** can be read (they provide background or supplementary context) – they are *not* part of the main narrative order but are highly recommended for additional insight:

* **The Gold Dinar & Silver Dirham: Islam and the Future of Money**. Discusses Islamic monetary history and its eschatological significance.
* **The Caliphate, the Hijaz & the Saudi-Wahhabi Nation-State**. Explores how the Ottoman/Saudi caliphate fits into modern End-Times prophecies.
* **Signs of the Last Day in the Modern Age**. Surveys contemporary “signs” of \<mark>Qiyāmah;\</mark> originally listed as Book 1 in his works.


**Sources:**  Shikh Imran N Hosein’s official e-book listings and book catalog, which enumerate all of the above titles.
